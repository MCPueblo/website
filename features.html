Technical Info

  • MCPueblo's primary host is SlothHost, on their Premium 8 GB plan with an Intel Xeon E-2288G (i9-9900K equivalent) and NVMe SSD.
  • The website and Discord bot is also hosted on SlothHost, the former on a 2 GB VPS, the latter on a dedicated Discord Bot hosting plan.
  • Backups are produced every 24 hours at 6:30 AM EST to a dedicated 1 TB backup server.
  • Many of our projects, including this website, are open source!
